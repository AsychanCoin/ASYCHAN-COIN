# ASN Governance Framework

## Overview

Governance allows ASN holders to participate in ecosystem decision-making.

## Governance Objectives

* Transparency
* Decentralization
* Community Participation
* Long-Term Sustainability

## Voting Rights

ASN holders may vote on:

* Treasury Spending
* Partnerships
* Ecosystem Upgrades
* Governance Proposals

## Proposal Process

1. Community Discussion
2. Proposal Submission
3. Review Period
4. Voting Period
5. Execution

## Future DAO

ASN intends to transition toward a decentralized autonomous organization (DAO) model governed by the community.
