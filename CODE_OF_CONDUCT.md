# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in the ASN community a harassment-free experience for everyone regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, nationality, race, religion, or experience level.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful.
- Be constructive.
- Welcome new contributors.
- Focus on community growth.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or discrimination.
- Personal attacks.
- Trolling.
- Doxxing.
- Offensive language.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct.

Violations may result in warnings, temporary suspension, or permanent bans.
